# Stop The War!

Please consider donating to these organisations helping people on the
ground in Ukraine and beyond, with focus on LGBTQIA+ and BIPoC:

* [Each One Teach One e.V.](https://eoto-archiv.de/spenden/) -- Community-based education and empowerment project in Berlin.\
* [Quarteera e.V.](http://www.quarteera.de) -- direct help for LGBTQIA+ in
Ukraine.

Also consider donating in favor of:

- [All Out](https://campaigns.allout.org/ukraine-fundraiser) -- direct
help for LGBTQIA+ in Ukraine.\
- [Black Foreigners in
Ukraine](https://linktr.ee/BlackforeignersInUkraine) -- direct help for
BIPoC in Ukraine.\
- [FONDAMENT gGmbH](https://fondament.de/) -- support of charitable
action.\
- [∄ Community Fund](https://k41community.fund/) -- direct help for
Kyiv\'s music and cultural community.
